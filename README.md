# My Favorite Movie: Batman

**Genre:** Action, Crime, Superhero  

*Batman* has always been one of my favorite movies because it combines mystery, justice, and an incredible hero who relies on intelligence and courage instead of superpowers. The dark atmosphere and complex storytelling make it stand out among other superhero films.

## Main Characters
- **Bruce Wayne / Batman**
- **Alfred Pennyworth**
- **Commissioner Gordon**
- **Selina Kyle (Catwoman)**
- **The Joker**

## Why I Like It
I admire Batman because he represents perseverance and justice. Despite his fears and losses, he continues to fight for what’s right — showing that anyone can be a hero through determination and compassion.

> “It’s not who I am underneath, but what I do that defines me.” — *Batman*

### Fun Fact
The Bat-Signal was first introduced in *Detective Comics #60* (1942) and has since become one of the most iconic superhero symbols in history.
